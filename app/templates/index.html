FAQ

What is VADATHON?
A 48-hour high-performance hackathon focused on the intersection of hardware and software. It's where bits meet atoms.
Who can join?
Builders of all kinds. Whether you're a firmware specialist, a web dev, or a mechanical engineer, there's a place for you here.
Is it free?
Yes. We provide the workspace, the hardware kits, the food, and the mentorship. You provide the innovation.
Where is it located?
VADATHON is a hybrid event with physical hubs in major tech centers and a global virtual participation platform.
